Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our spice export services

What is your minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type. Most whole spices require 50-100kg minimum, while ground spices start at 500kg. Mixed container orders are welcome with a total minimum order value of $10,000 USD.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) with 30% advance payment and 70% before shipment, or Letter of Credit (L/C) at sight for orders over $50,000 USD. Small orders under $5,000 require 100% advance payment.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery time is 7-21 days after payment confirmation, depending on order size and destination. Stock items ship within 7-14 days, while made-to-order products require 21-30 days. Organic certified products may need an additional 7-14 days.

Do you provide organic certified spices?

Yes, we offer USDA and EU organic certified spices. Our organic products are sourced from certified farms and processed in our organic-certified facilities. Organic certification documentation is available upon request.

What quality certifications do you have?

We are certified with ISO 22000:2018, HACCP, GMP, and Halal certifications. Our quality management system ensures consistent product quality and food safety standards that meet international requirements.

Can you provide samples before ordering?

Yes, we provide samples for quality evaluation. Sample costs and shipping charges apply, which can be credited against your first order. Contact our sales team to request samples of specific products.

What shipping terms do you offer?

We offer FOB Indonesian ports, CIF destination ports, CFR destination ports, and EXW factory terms. FOB is most common for container shipments. Our team can help you choose the best shipping option for your needs.
