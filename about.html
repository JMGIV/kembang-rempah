Our Story

Founded in 1999, Kembang Rempah emerged from a deep passion for Indonesia's rich spice heritage and a vision to share these extraordinary flavors with the world. What began as a small family operation has grown into one of Indonesia's most trusted spice exporters.

Our name, "Kembang" reflects our commitment to the entire Indonesian archipelago - from the cinnamon forests of Sumatra to the clove plantations of Sulawesi, and the legendary nutmeg islands of Maluku. We work directly with over 1,200 farming families, ensuring fair trade practices and sustainable cultivation methods.

Today, we export more than 15,000 tons of premium spices annually to 45 countries worldwide, maintaining the same dedication to quality and authenticity that has defined our journey from the beginning.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming Indonesia's premier spice exporter

1999 - Foundation

Kembang Rempah was founded by the Wijaya family in Jakarta with a mission to bring authentic Indonesian spices to international markets. Starting with just three spice varieties and a handful of local farmers.

2005 - First Export

Our first major export shipment of 50 tons of premium cloves to Europe marked the beginning of our international expansion. This milestone established our reputation for quality and reliability.

2010 - Quality Certification

Achieved ISO 22000 and HACCP certifications, demonstrating our commitment to food safety and quality management. This opened doors to premium markets in North America and Europe.

2015 - Sustainable Partnerships

Launched our farmer partnership program, working directly with over 500 farming families to ensure sustainable practices and fair trade. Introduced organic certification for select products.

2020 - Digital Transformation

Embraced digital innovation with advanced tracking systems, online ordering platform, and real-time quality monitoring. Expanded to serve 35 countries despite global challenges.

2025 - Global Leader

Today, we export over 15,000 tons annually to 45 countries, working with 1,200+ farming families. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and authentic Indonesian flavors remains stronger than ever.

Sustainable Partnerships

Our commitment extends beyond quality spices to the wellbeing of our farming communities and the preservation of Indonesia's rich agricultural heritage. We work directly with over 1,200 farming families across the archipelago.

Through our sustainable partnership program, we provide technical assistance, fair pricing, and long-term contracts that ensure stable incomes for farming communities. This approach has helped preserve traditional cultivation methods while improving yields and quality.

Our organic certification program supports farmers in transitioning to sustainable practices, reducing environmental impact while meeting growing global demand for organic spices. We believe that what's good for the planet is good for business.
